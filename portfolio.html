Of the Universe


(27 ½ in * 19 ¾ in)

I am standing in the midst of stillness.
The stillness surrounds me, submerges my whole body; I am inside the stillness.
I no longer know what part of me is or is not part of the universe.
Every atom in my body is from a collapsing star.
In the expanse of space, atoms were fused and elements formed – I was created.
My mind and body all began from the pursing star; I am of the universe.

-inspired by Anis Mojgani

Life Forms


(19 ¾ in * 24 ½ in)

Manmade construction, garbage, and pollution have destroyed the earth.
Humans change every place they invade.
The forest that was once a clean habitat for wildlife ceases to exist.
Humans cannot fully recover nature from the disaster they created.
Yet, still nature works to recover itself – life forms even from the trash heaps.

Light Shines


(24 ½ in * 19 ¾ in)

When I paint, I usually start with white paper and coat the paper with darkness.
For this drawing, everything started from the darkness.
It is from the darkness that light was painted.
